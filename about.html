Professional journey

Originally trained in chemical engineering and materials science, I moved into product management because I wanted to work closer to user problems, business decisions, and the teams that turn ideas into shipped products.

With 5+ years of experience, I have worked on SaaS platforms, internal data products, and web applications across B2B2C and B2B business models. My work spans UX research, user acceptance testing, data-driven experimentation, roadmap planning, and product-led growth.

How I work

Product ownership

I bring structure to the product lifecycle, from problem framing through iteration, launch, and continuous improvement.

Strategic planning

I like connecting near-term delivery to longer-range roadmaps, especially when customer insight and business goals need translation.

Customer and market insight

I use interviews, qualitative synthesis, behavioral data, and stakeholder input to prioritize features that solve real user problems.

Experimentation

My research background shaped how I define hypotheses, success metrics, and learning loops for product decisions.

Technical fluency

I am comfortable working with technical teams and data-heavy products where precision and user clarity both matter.

Continued learning

I earned an MBA from NYU Stern with a focus on Technical Product Management and Management of Technology and Operations.
